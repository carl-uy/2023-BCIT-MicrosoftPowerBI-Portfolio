
# Customer and Employee Data

This folder contains datasets related to customers and employees. The data is used for various analyses including customer segmentation, employee territory management, and performance tracking.

## Files Included:
1. **Customer_Data.csv** - Contains customer information including IDs, company names, contact information, and other relevant details.
2. **Employee_Info.csv** - Details of employees such as IDs, names, titles, and contact details.
3. **Employee_Territories.csv** - Mapping of employees to their respective territories.

## Usage:
These datasets are integral for understanding customer demographics and employee distribution. They can be used to analyze sales performance, customer relationship management, and workforce allocation.
